Cryptic

A revolutionary technology that will take the blockchain by storm.

We plan to create the ultimate currency by focusing on scalability, performance and innovation.

The following features are projected to be implemented by the end of 2018. See our roadmap for more details.

  • Hyperspeed Transactions

    Once development is complete, our network will be capable of performing 1 billion transactions per second.

  • Unlimited Hyperspeed Sidechains

    To maintain hyperspeed transactions, instead of building dApps on-chain, they will have their own chain, linked to cryptico using our chainlink technology.

  • Neural Networks

    In preparation of the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence, we have implemented the ability for neural network programming within dApps. The cryptico network will also have its own advanced neural networks.

  • True Privacy

    Transactions on the cryptico network will be completely private and untraceable using our spin technology. There will also be an option for public transactions.
